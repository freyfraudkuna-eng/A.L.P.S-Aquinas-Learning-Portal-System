Our Subjects

Mathematics

Problem solving and logical thinking are emphasized in every lesson.

Algebra, geometry, statistics, and calculus fundamentals are covered to strengthen critical thinking and analytical skills.

English

Develop communication, comprehension, and literature appreciation.

Focus on grammar, creative writing, reading comprehension, speech, and literary analysis to enhance language proficiency.

Science

Encourage exploration and understanding of the natural world.

Includes biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science with experiments and projects to develop inquiry-based learning.

Filipino

Study of language, culture, and communication skills.

Covers Filipino grammar, literature, oral communication, and appreciation of Philippine culture and history.

ICT

Develop computer literacy and technological skills.

Covers basic programming, digital literacy, computer applications, and safe internet practices.

TLE

Practical livelihood and technical skills for real-life application.

Includes cooking, woodworking, electronics, home economics, and other hands-on activities to build skills for future careers.

VE / CL

Character building and values education.

Covers moral development, community service, leadership, and decision-making skills to nurture responsible citizens.

MAPEH

Integration of music, arts, physical education, and health.

Encourages creativity, fitness, sportsmanship, personal hygiene, and overall wellness through practical and theoretical lessons.

Technical Writing

Develop professional writing skills for formal contexts.

Covers report writing, documentation, instructions, and other professional communication formats used in academic and workplace settings.

Araling Panlipunan

Study of Philippine society, history, and governance.

Includes Philippine history, geography, culture, government, and civic responsibilities to promote national awareness and social understanding.
